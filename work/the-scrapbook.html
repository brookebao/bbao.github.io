The Idea
In my senior year of high school, I collected many photos in my phone with family, friends, and places that IÂ knew would soon be far away. I wanted to remember the Starbucks trips before robotics, driving by the beach, Christmas with my family, etc. etc. I notice that even when good feelings last, the small details in good memories fade. So I wanted to capture all these memories in physical form. But photos are only snapshots of time...
The Execution
Using paper, patterns, color, and any physical remnants of that day, I printed out the photos and attempted to capture each memory as a memory in motion. I have shown some pages below in the hopes I have conveyed the integrity of each memory.
